The Project

My main responsibility over my year-long internship as associate artist at BioWare was to board out in-game cutscenes for Anthem, their newest online multiplayer action video game. I also completed miscellaneous 2D art as requested.

Storyboard examples are under NDA.

Roles

  • Storyboarding
  • Digital Art

Tools

  • Photoshop
  • After Effects
  • Frostbite

Storyboarding

After creating a first draft based on a script, I pitched the boards to a team of individuals from narrative, animation, art, and audio departments. The feedback I received was then incorporated into subsequent drafts. All storyboards went through multiple rounds of revision depending on the complexity of the scene. Completed storyboards were then passed on to other departments to carry out the next steps.

For some scenes I put my boards together into animatics. These rough animatics were a way to gauge timing and pacing of the scene, and were also another format used for presentation.

Reflection

The learning experience was a very valuable part of my internship. I learned a lot about the different teams and the collaborative nature of triple A game development.
